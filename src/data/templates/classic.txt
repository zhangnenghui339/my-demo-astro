 CAREERRADAR TEMPLATE 01 - ATS CLASSIC (UNIVERSAL)
 Single-column layout that parses cleanly in every major ATS
============================================================
 HOW TO USE
 1. Replace every [BRACKETED] field with your own content,
    then delete any section you don't need.
 2. Keep the single-column structure. No tables, text boxes,
    columns, photos, or icons.
 3. Keep the section titles exactly as written - parsers are
    trained on these standard headings.
 4. Paste into Word/Google Docs, set font to Calibri or
    Arial 11pt, save as .docx or export a text-based PDF.
 5. Verify the final file with the free ATS checker at
    https://careerradar.io
============================================================
